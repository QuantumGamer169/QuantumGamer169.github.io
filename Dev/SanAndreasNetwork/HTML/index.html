FAQ

How do i apply for moderator?

How to apply for moderator please head your way to the application section within our website and press the moderation section and a form will pop up for you to apply But Staff application are only open on the first week every month.

How to apply for a police/fire department

How to apply for a police/fire department please head your way to the application section within our website and press department section and apply for the department you wish to apply for. Departments are always open.

Can I apply for multiple departments?

You may be apart of a police department and a fire department. Police departments are classed as a main departments and fire/ems departments are classed as mini departments. So you can apply for one Main department and mini department.

How do i report a player?

You can create a moderation ticket or do /report (ID) (Reason) in game and a staff member will help you as soon as possible.

How do i get unbanned from SANRP?

If you wish to be unbanned head your way to the appeal section and fill out the form and you will get a response within one week to let you know if your appeal has been approved or denied.

How many people actively play on the server?

Due to we are a new server and we started in May 10th 2022 we can not give an answer to that question at this time.

Do we host Server Events?

Yes we host server events we are open for suggestions for events or any ideas you have in mind. If you would like to recommend a event open a general question ticket.

How do i Donate?

You can head your way to the donation section on the website and it will late you to the donation page and it will show you benefits you can get with donating.
