About Me

Hi, I’m Josh! I work for an MSP in Lincolnshire, helping businesses get the IT tools they need to thrive. I’m passionate about all things tech—from gaming and programming to infrastructure. Currently, I’m working toward my bachelor’s degree with the goal of starting my own IT company.

I’m driven by efficiency and prefer to dive in and get things done. Outside of work, I love working on cars—though I don’t get to do it often, my dream is to buy and restore cheap exotic cars one day.

I’m also on the autism spectrum, which gives me a unique approach to problem-solving and understanding systems. I embrace this as a strength in both my work and personal life.

Whether you need IT support or just want to chat about tech (or cars!), I’m always up for a conversation. Let’s get things done and have fun along the way!
